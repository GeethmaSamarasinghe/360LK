OUR STORY

What we do?

360°LK lets you explore Sri Lanka from home. You can visit amazing places without leaving your home.


With our platform, you can:

  • Walk through ancient ruins and beautiful beaches
  • Use arrow keys to move around, just like in a video game
  • Learn about Sri Lankan culture and history
  • See wildlife and natural wonders
  • Get ideas for your future trip to Sri Lanka

We work with local experts to make sure our tours are authentic and accurate. We want to share the real Sri Lanka with you.

Whether you're planning a trip,learning about new places, or just curious about Sri Lanka- we make it easy and fun to explore this beautiful country.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the leading platform for virtual tourism in Sri Lanka, connecting people worldwide to the country's natural, cultural, and historical treasures. We aim to inspire curiosity, promote sustainable travel, and make immersive experiences accessible to everyone,regardless of location, time, or financial constraints.

Mission

360°LK bridges technology and travel by providing high-quality, interactive virtual tours of Sri Lanka's destinations. Our platform delivers educational, cultural, and recreational value through 360° videos, live-guided tours, and VR experiences. We strive to support local tourism, empower communities, and offer users a seamless digital travel experience that encourages real-worls exploration while minimizing environmental impact.
