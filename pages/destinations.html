DESTINATIONS

Browse 50+ Sri Lankan destinations through immersive 360° virtual tours and discover the island in a whole new way. Move freely through each location, explore hidden corners, and experience the beauty of Sri Lanka as if you're standing there in person.

History lover? Step into ancient cities, temples, and archaeological wonders.

Beach seeker? Glide through golden shores, calm bays, and crystal-blue coastlines.

Nature enthusiast? Explore forests, waterfalls, and wildlife-rich national parks.

NATURE

Yala National Park

Yala National Park is one of Sri Lanka's top wildlife hotspots, famous for leopards and elephants. Its diverse landscapes make every safari a unique adventure.

View Tour

Horton Plains

Horton Plains is a misty highland plateau known for its breathtaking “World's End” viewpoint. It offers scenic trails surrounded by rare flora and fauna.

View Tour

Sinharaja Rain Forest

Sinharaja is Sri Lanka's last remaining primary rainforest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Its rich biodiversity makes it a paradise for nature lovers and researchers.

CULTURE

❮

The Nallur Kandaswami Kovil

Nallur Kovil is one of the most sacred Hindu temples in Sri Lanka, known for its grand architecture. The temple's vibrant festivals attract thousands of devotees every year.

View Tour

The Dambulla Cave Temple

The Dambulla Cave Temple is Sri Lanka's largest and best-preserved cave temple complex. Its ancient murals and Buddha statues make it a key cultural treasure.

View Tour

Anuradhapura Sandakadapahana

The Sandakadapahana is an iconic carved moonstone of Anuradhapura, symbolizing the Buddhist cycle of life.

HISTORICAL

❮

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress known for its stunning frescoes and engineering brilliance. The climb reveals breathtaking views and a deep glimpse into Sri Lanka's royal history.

View Tour

The Royal Palace in Polonnaruwa

The Royal Palace in Polonnaruwa showcases the architectural grandeur of ancient Sri Lankan kings. Its ruins reflect advanced craftsmanship and the power of the Polonnaruwa kingdom.

View Tour

King Parakramabahu Statue

A famed ancient statue believed to depict King Parakramabahu, symbolizing his wisdom and vision for prosperity.

BEACHES

❮

Arugambe Beach

Arugambe is a world-renowned surfing beach with golden sands and relaxing coastal vibes. It attracts surfers, backpackers, and nature lovers from around the world.

View Tour

Koggala Beach

Koggala Beach is a quiet and scenic stretch of coastline ideal for calm getaways. The logoon and nearby stilt fishermen add to its natural charm.

View Tour

Benthota Beach

Bentota Beach is known for its wide sandy shores and clear waters perfect for water sports. It is one of Sri Lanka's most popular resort areas for relaxation and adventure.

View Tour

CITIES

❮

Colombo

Colombo is Sri Lanka's bustling commercial capital, known for its modern skyline and vibrant city life. It blends colonial landmarks, shopping districts, and coastal views into one lively urban experience.

View Tour

Galle

Galle is famous for its historic Dutch Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city offers charming streets, seaside walls, and a unique mix of culture and architecture.

View Tour

Kandy

Kandy is Sri Lanka's cultural heart, surrounded by mountains and lush greenery. It is home to the Temple of the Tooth, one of the most sacred Buddhist sites in the world.
